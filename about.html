About Norway

The country of Vikings and Valkyries
Norway is attractive with ski resorts and exotic Arctic regions.

In the period from the end of May to the middle of August, thanks to the warm current of the "Gulf stream", Norway is turning into a country of beach holidays.

A fantastic country that can charm with it's coolness, fresh air and amazing nature. Local fjords impenetrable forests are striking in their beauty, and legends about Vikings and Valkyries cause delight.
